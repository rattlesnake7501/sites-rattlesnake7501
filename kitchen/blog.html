Sara's Fiery Anger

Today, I reached my limit. I have been living with Mikula for months now and I'm tired of cleaning up after him all the time. It all came to a head when I confronted him about the pile of dirty dishes he left in the sink from last night's dinner.

He was completely dismissive, claiming he was going to clean them up later. Later! It's been over 12 hours and he hasn't lifted a finger.

I was so angry that I couldn't contain myself. As I stood in the kitchen, flames started to flicker around my body and I rose off the ground. Mikula was shocked and scared, but I was beyond reason. I screamed at him that I had enough and that I was flying into the sun.

And that's exactly what I did. I flew higher and higher, my anger propelling me towards the sun. I could feel the heat and the power of it all around me. It was the ultimate release of my pent-up frustration.

But as I got closer, I realized that I was really going to fly into the sun. And I didn't care. I was done with Mikula, with the dishes, with all of it. I closed my eyes and let the sun consume me.

And that's where I am now, forever a part of the fiery energy that powers our universe. Good riddance to Mikula and all his dirty dishes.
