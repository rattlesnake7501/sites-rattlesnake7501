Welcome to Computer Science


Computer Science offers courses concerned with the design and maintenance of systems and setting up wired and wireless networks.
You will develop programming skills and techniques for use in software engineering, networking and web development industries.
Enterprise, work experience and visits play an important part in ensuring students develop cutting-edge skills that are relevant and valued by employers.
State-of-the-art equipment will be available to you – there are over 3,000 workstations with multimedia and internet facilities across the College's main campuses,
well equipped teaching suites and access to specialist computing suites at various sites.
This department has established strong links with the technology industry including running a Microsoft Academy and our staff,
who are armed with extensive industry experience, are able to help develop your skills in preparation for a career in the sector.
