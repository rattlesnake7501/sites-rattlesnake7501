Welcome to Avaiation


The course is designed for students wishing to follow careers within the aviation industry or for students wishing to go on to Higher Education.
The course also involves the use of the College Air Cabin Simulator as well as the training facilities we have at Leeds Bradford Airport,
giving you an excellent insight into the industry when seeking employment or further study.
There will also be the opportunity to undertake the Fire and Smoke Training and Wet Drills training.
