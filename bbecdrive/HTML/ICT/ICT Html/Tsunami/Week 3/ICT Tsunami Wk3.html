Tsunami - 5 Week Module
Week 3

Starter

 Watch the video about "What is Photoshop" and who uses it.

 

Task 1

 

 Introduction into Fireworks or Photoshop. Show pupils how to make an A4 canvas 29cm L x 21cm W. Give them a tour of the software and what it can do. "WAGGOL sample"

 

Task 2

 

 Students will follow a step by step video on how to make a basic poster.

 

Task 3

 

Students will continue making their posters. Remind them their audience is everyone young & old. The purpose of the poster is to warn or inform people about Tsunamis.
Homework Create a logo for your posters called "Tsunami Watch".

You have this lesson to complete all tasks

Tsunami Poster

Gathering a range of factual information on Tsunamis

Design the poster layout in Fireworks or Photoshop

Continue to design the poster layout in Fireworks or Photoshop

Reflect on what you have learned over the course of study and apply it to your presentation.
