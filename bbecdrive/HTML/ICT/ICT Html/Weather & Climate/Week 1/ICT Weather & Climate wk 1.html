Weather & Climate - 6 Week Module
Week 1

Starter

 Find ten facts about the weather!

 

Task 1

 

 Introduction into weather & Climate unit. Students create Weather & Climate folder then download zip folder into it.

 

Task 2

 

Teacher now explains how we gather data and make it useful by organising it. Using spread sheets is one way of doing this. Then introduce them to spread sheets & how they work.

 

Task 3

 

Pupils use downloaded spread sheet and start to add their own look to it. They must add colour, highlight important cell in black boarders, merge cells for a titles and insert a picture.
