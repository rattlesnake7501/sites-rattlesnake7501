Regeneration - 7 Week Module
Week 2

Starter

 Discussion of the different types of regeneration that has been taking place in East London. Relate this back to the poster they are creating.

 

Task 1

 

Pupils will make a start on their poster on the benefits East London will receive from the 2012 Olympics. Use Adobe Photoshop and create new document - (A4) 21cm wide X 29 cm length

 

Task 2

 

 Pupils swap seats to evaluate each others work using the spreadsheet tick list.

 

Task 3

 

Pupils to take on board improvements and make any necessary changes to poster before submitting finish work in the form of a JPEG in their postbox.
